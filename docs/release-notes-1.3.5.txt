APPLE APP STORE
---------------

What's New in v1.3.5

Tasks & Reminders
BreedWise now tracks what needs doing, not just what happened.
A new task system replaces the old dashboard alerts: every
breeding record automatically creates a pregnancy-check follow-up,
and you can add your own manual reminders for any mare or stallion.
Tasks appear on the home screen and route you directly to the right
form when you tap them.

Sticky Follow-Up Bar
After saving a daily log, a persistent action bar appears at the
bottom of the screen so you can immediately jump to a breeding
record, pregnancy check, or flush form — no hunting through menus.

Daily Log: Fluid Tracking
You can now record uterine fluid presence and grade directly on
the daily log. When fluid is detected, a flush follow-up option
appears automatically.

Breeding Event Detail View
Tap any breeding record card to open a full detail view —
method, dates, stallion, frozen batch details, and timestamps
all in one place.

Breeding Record Timestamps
Breeding records now capture and display created and updated
timestamps, giving you a clearer audit trail.

Multiple Daily Checks Per Mare
You can log more than one ultrasound per mare per day — useful
for mares being scanned morning and afternoon during peak
follicle growth.

Collection Wizard Improvements
The add-collection flow is now fully scrollable on smaller
screens, and includes a new Motility (%) field for fresh and
cooled semen evaluations.

12h / 24h Clock Setting
Choose your preferred time format under Settings. The selection
applies everywhere times are displayed in the app.

Cleaner Forms
Optional fields no longer show "(optional)" placeholder text —
forms are quieter and easier to scan.

Bug Fixes & Polish
- Fixed daily log wizard hardware-back navigation on Android
- Corrected ovary display formatting in daily log review step
- Stability improvements throughout

SHORT DESCRIPTION (72 / 80 characters)
---------------------------------------

Horse breeding & reproductive records: mares, stallions, foals. Offline.


LONG DESCRIPTION (up to 4,000 characters)
------------------------------------------

BreedWise is the equine breeding log built for working horse breeders —
not a generic livestock app adapted for horses. Every screen is designed
around mare reproductive cycles, stallion breeding records, and foal
tracking, so you spend less time managing the app and more time in the barn.

BUILT FOR THE BREEDING SHED

Log daily ultrasound exams with structured fields for follicle size, edema
grade, uterine tone, and fluid presence. The daily log wizard walks you
through each scan step by step and flags flush follow-ups automatically
when fluid is detected. Record multiple checks per mare per day — essential
during peak follicle growth when you're scanning morning and afternoon.

FULL MARE REPRODUCTIVE RECORDS

Every mare has her own profile with swipeable tabs covering the full
reproductive cycle:
• Daily logs with ovary and uterine exam data
• Breeding records (live cover, cooled, and frozen AI)
• Pregnancy checks with days post-ovulation and estimated due dates
• Foaling records with outcome tracking
• Foal profiles with milestone checklists (stood, nursed, IgG tested, and more)
• Medications log

STALLION RECORDS & FROZEN SEMEN INVENTORY

Track stallion breeding history alongside frozen semen inventory. Each
semen batch stores collection date, motility, concentration, straw count,
volume, and storage location. The collection wizard guides you through
fresh, cooled, and frozen evaluations. Straw counts are tracked at the
batch level so you always know what inventory remains.

TASKS & FOLLOW-UP REMINDERS

BreedWise generates tasks automatically. Breeding a mare creates a
pregnancy-check follow-up in the task list. Add manual reminders for any
mare or stallion. The home screen shows open tasks and routes you directly
to the right form when you tap one — no hunting through menus.

DESIGNED TO STAY OUT OF YOUR WAY

• Fully offline — all data lives on your device, no account required
• Configurable 12h or 24h time display
• Breeding event detail view for a complete record at a glance
• Breeding and collection timestamps for a clear audit trail
• Sticky follow-up action bar after daily logs so your next step is one tap away

WHO USES BREEDWISE

Warmblood, Thoroughbred, Quarter Horse, and sport horse breeders managing
their own mares. Embryo transfer programs tracking multiple recipients.
Stallion owners recording outside-mare services. Breeding managers at
small private farms who need accurate records without a full farm-management
subscription.

Your horses' reproductive history is too important to trust to spreadsheets
or paper. BreedWise keeps it organized, on your phone, and always with you.
